Merbok Express

Ride Experience

The Merbok Express is a delightful ride that takes you on a scenic tour through beautifully crafted landscapes and enchanting scenes. Perfect for families and guests of all ages, this ride provides a relaxing and enjoyable experience.

  • Scenic Tour: Enjoy a leisurely ride through picturesque settings, including lush gardens and whimsical scenes.
  • Comfortable Seating: The ride offers comfortable seating arrangements ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience.
  • Enchanting Scenes: Experience the magic of various themed areas with detailed decorations and interactive elements.
  • Relaxing Atmosphere: A peaceful ride that allows you to take in the beauty of the park's surroundings.

Rider Information

  • Height Requirement: No height requirement; suitable for all ages.
  • Duration: The ride lasts approximately 5 minutes.
  • Accessibility: The ride is accessible to guests with disabilities. Please check with park staff for assistance.
  • Location: The Merbok Express is located near the entrance of the park, making it easily accessible for all visitors.

Fun Facts

  • The Merbok Express is themed around the mythical bird "Merbok," known for its beauty and grace.
  • The ride features over 10 different themed areas, each with unique decorations and interactive elements.
  • It's one of the most popular family attractions in the park, often enjoyed multiple times by guests during their visit.
