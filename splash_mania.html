Splash Mania

Ride Experience

Splash Mania is an exhilarating water attraction that promises endless fun for visitors of all ages. Dive into a world of excitement with wave pools, thrilling slides, and interactive water play areas.

Key Features:

  • Wave Pool: Experience the thrill of the ocean with waves that vary in intensity, providing fun for both novice and experienced swimmers.
  • Thrilling Slides: High-speed water slides that twist and turn, offering a heart-pounding ride from start to finish.
  • Interactive Play Areas: Engage with a variety of water features, including water cannons, splash pads, and tipping buckets, perfect for younger visitors.
  • Relaxation Zones: Float along the lazy river or lounge in our shaded seating areas for a more relaxing experience.

Rider Information:

  • Height Requirement: No height restrictions for the wave pool and interactive play areas. Some slides have a minimum height requirement of 120 cm.
  • Duration: Visitors can enjoy Splash Mania for as long as the park is open.
  • Accessibility: The attraction is accessible to guests with disabilities. Please check with park staff for assistance.
  • Location: Splash Mania is located in the Water Zone, easily accessible from the main entrance.

Fun Facts:

  • Splash Mania features one of the largest wave pools in the region, capable of generating waves up to 1.5 meters high.
  • The attraction includes over 10 different water slides, each offering a unique and thrilling experience.
  • Interactive play areas are designed with safety in mind, featuring soft surfaces and shallow water levels.
  • Splash Mania is a favorite among families, providing activities suitable for all ages.
