Park Rules and Regulations


Welcome to our Merbok Land! To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone, please familiarize yourself with the following rules and regulations.

Allowed Outfits

  • Comfortable clothing appropriate for the weather.
  • Closed-toe shoes for safety on rides.
  • Swimwear only in designated water park areas.
  • Costumes during special events, provided they are family-friendly.

Not Allowed Outfits

  • Clothing with offensive language or graphics.
  • Excessively torn or revealing clothing.
  • Loose accessories that could pose a safety risk on rides.
  • Swimwear outside designated water park areas.

General Guidelines

  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • No outside food or beverages allowed, except for medical or infant needs.
  • Smoking is only permitted in designated areas.
  • Respect other guests and park staff.
  • Follow all ride-specific safety instructions and height requirements.

Prohibited Items

  • Weapons of any kind.
  • Drugs or illegal substances.
  • Glass containers.
  • Drones or other remote-controlled devices.
