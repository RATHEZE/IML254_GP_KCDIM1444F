Merbok Mountain Flume

Ride Experience

The Merbok Mountain Flume is a captivating water ride that takes you through a breathtaking mountainous landscape. The ride features exhilarating drops, winding waterways, and immersive thematic scenery, providing an unforgettable adventure for the whole family.

  • Water Adventure: Splash through winding waterways and experience the thrill of unexpected turns and drops.
  • Scenic Views: Enjoy the beautiful mountain-themed scenery with detailed decorations and natural elements.
  • Exciting Drops: Feel the rush as you plunge down steep drops and navigate through rapid waters.
  • Immersive Experience: The ride features interactive elements and thematic decorations that enhance the adventure.

Rider Information

  • Height Requirement: Minimum height of 90 cm. Riders under 120 cm must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Duration: The ride lasts approximately 6 minutes.
  • Accessibility: The ride is accessible to guests with disabilities. Please check with park staff for assistance.
  • Location: The Merbok Mountain Flume is located in the Adventure Zone, easily accessible from the main entrance.

Fun Facts

  • The Merbok Mountain Flume is inspired by the mythical Merbok bird, known for its majestic beauty and adventurous spirit.
  • The ride features over 15 different themed areas, each with unique decorations and interactive elements.
  • The flume was designed by renowned theme park architects, ensuring a safe and thrilling experience for all guests.
  • It's one of the most popular water rides in the park, offering a perfect blend of excitement and scenic beauty.
