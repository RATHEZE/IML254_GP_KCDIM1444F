Admission Rates(MYR)
Adult(13 years old and above) RM250
Child(12 years old and above) RM200
Senior Citizen(60 years old and above) RM200



Important Note:

  • All tickets are valid only for the date and time specified at the time of purchase. Tickets are non-transferable and cannot be rescheduled.
  • Guests must present a valid ticket (either printed or digital) at the entrance. Photo ID may be required for verification.
  • Senior Citizen rates apply for age 60 and above - proof of age required.
  • Tickets are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged once purchased. In the event of park closure due to weather or unforeseen circumstances, guests will be offered an alternative date.
  • Appropriate swimwear is mandatory for all water attractions. For safety reasons, no loose clothing, accessories, or metal objects are allowed on rides.
  • Outside food and beverages, glass containers, and sharp objects are not permitted inside the park. Bags may be checked at the entrance.
  • Other terms & conditions apply.
