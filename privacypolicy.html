Privacy Policy



Thank you for visiting Merbok Land . This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from visitors to our Site. By using our Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.


1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing information when you purchase tickets, make reservations, or contact us through the Site.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information about your interactions with the Site, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit.
  • Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze how the Site is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: To process ticket purchases, requests, and reservations, and to communicate with you about your transactions.
  • Marketing: With your consent, to send you promotional emails and newsletters about our waterpark and related offers.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Site or providing services to you (e.g., payment processors, marketing platforms).
  • We may disclose your information when required by law, to protect our rights, or to respond to legal requests.

4. Your Choices

  • You can choose not to provide certain personal information, although this may limit your ability to use certain features of the Site.
  • By following the instructions in our emails or contacting us directly, you can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us.

5. Data Security

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and use.

6. Children's Privacy

  • Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents of children under 13 who believe their child has provided us with personal information may contact us to request deletion.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
