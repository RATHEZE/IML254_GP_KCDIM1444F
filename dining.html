Restaurant

Merbok Meadows Cafe

Merbok Meadows Cafe offers specialty coffees, freshly baked pastries, artisanal sandwiches, and gourmet salads in a cozy atmosphere with rustic decor and lush green plants. Enjoy weekend acoustic sessions and soothing live music.

Merbok Mosaic Cafe

Merbok Mosaic Cafe transports you to a culinary paradise with tropical-inspired dishes, refreshing fruit smoothies, and tropical cocktails. The vibrant, colorful decor and comfortable bamboo furniture create a mini-vacation ambiance.

Merbok Glade Cafe

Merbok Glade Cafe offers a diverse menu inspired by international cuisines. Enjoy juicy burgers, flavorful stir-fries, and authentic street food. Don't miss their signature Adventure Sundae and eclectic travel-themed decor.

Merbok Merriments Cafe

Merbok Merriments Cafe is a delightful haven with an eclectic mix of comfort food and gourmet treats. Enjoy fluffy pancakes, avocado toast, hand-crafted pizzas, gourmet burgers, and fresh salads. The vibrant atmosphere is perfect for memorable moments and joyful gatherings.
