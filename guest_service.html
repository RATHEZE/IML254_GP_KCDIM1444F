Welcome to Guest Services

Our Guest Services team is dedicated to ensuring that your visit to our theme park is enjoyable, comfortable, and hassle-free. From stroller rentals to lost and found services, we offer a range of amenities and assistance to enhance your experience.

Explore our various services below to learn more about how we can make your visit more convenient and memorable:

Make your visit more convenient with our stroller rental service. Here's what you need to know:

  • Single and double strollers available for rent near the park entrance.
  • Comfortable and sturdy strollers suitable for children of all ages.
  • Easy rental process with affordable rates.
  • Strollers undergo regular maintenance and cleaning for hygiene and safety.

Renting a stroller allows you to explore the park with ease, ensuring a comfortable experience for both you and your little ones.

Stay cash-ready with our conveniently located ATMs throughout the park. Here's what you need to know:

  • Multiple ATMs available at strategic locations for easy access.
  • No need to worry about running out of cash during your visit.
  • ATMs accept major credit and debit cards for withdrawals.
  • Ensure a hassle-free experience by having cash on hand for purchases and transactions.

Accessing cash has never been easier with our on-site ATM services, providing peace of mind for your park experience.

Lost something during your visit? Don't worry, our Lost & Found service is here to help:

  • Report lost items at the Guest Services desk located near the park entrance.
  • Our dedicated team will assist you in locating and retrieving your lost belongings.
  • Found items are securely stored and logged for easy retrieval.
  • Contact Guest Services for assistance in reclaiming your lost items.

Rest assured that we'll do our best to reunite you with your lost items and ensure a memorable experience at our park.

We strive to make our park accessible to all visitors. Here are the services we offer for disabled individuals:

  • Wheelchair rental services available for visitors with mobility impairments.
  • Accessible pathways and facilities throughout the park to accommodate wheelchairs and mobility devices.
  • Assistance from park staff for boarding rides and accessing attractions.
  • Designated parking spaces close to the park entrance for individuals with disabilities.
  • Accessible restrooms equipped with facilities for disabled individuals.

We are committed to providing a safe and enjoyable experience for all visitors, regardless of their abilities.

Make your visit hassle-free with our convenient parking services:

  • Ample parking spaces available for visitors' vehicles.
  • Designated parking areas for cars, motorcycles, and oversized vehicles.
  • Accessible parking spaces close to the park entrance for disabled individuals.
  • Well-lit parking lots with security patrols for enhanced safety.
  • Convenient payment options including cash, credit/debit cards, and contactless payments.

Enjoy peace of mind knowing that your vehicle is securely parked while you explore the park's attractions.

Keep your belongings secure with our locker rental service:

  • Lockers available in various sizes to accommodate small bags, backpacks, and larger items.
  • Secure electronic lockers equipped with PIN codes for easy access.
  • Convenient locations near major attractions and facilities for quick access to stored items.
  • Affordable rental rates with options for hourly or daily rentals.
  • Peace of mind knowing your valuables are safe while you enjoy the park's offerings.

Renting a locker allows you to store your belongings safely and enjoy a worry-free experience throughout your visit.

Capture magical moments with our professional photography services:

  • Professional photographers stationed at key locations throughout the park to capture memorable moments.
  • On-ride photos available for purchase as souvenirs of your thrilling experiences.
  • Photo packages offering digital downloads, prints, and customized photo products.
  • Opportunities for character meet-and-greets with photo opportunities for the whole family.

Relive your park adventures with high-quality photos capturing every smile and laughter.

Bring your furry friends along for the fun with our pet-friendly amenities:

  • Designated pet-friendly areas within the park for your pets to stretch their legs and enjoy the outdoors.
  • Guidelines for bringing pets into the park, including leash requirements and pet waste disposal instructions.
  • Water stations and rest areas for pets to stay hydrated and comfortable throughout the day.
  • Pet care services available, including pet sitting and grooming facilities, for your convenience.

Make lasting memories with your pets as you explore the park together.

Stay connected throughout your visit with our complimentary Wi-Fi access:

  • Free Wi-Fi hotspots strategically located throughout the park for seamless connectivity.
  • High-speed internet access for browsing, social media updates, and sharing your park experiences online.
  • Instructions for accessing the Wi-Fi network and troubleshooting tips for common connectivity issues.

Share your park adventures instantly with friends and family, and stay connected to the digital world.
